ABOUT

My name is Toni Pandu Wijaya, I was born in Kuningan west java by a couple of Mr. Toto and Mrs. Nining, My life is almost the same as that of boys my age, so I don’t experience anything unique. At the age of seven, I studied at SDN 1 Citundun. There I was given the provision of character contoh autobiografi bahasa Inggris diri sendiri dan tokoh by the teachers so that I was able to adapt in people’s lives. Six years later, I graduated continuing my education at SMPN 2 Ciwaru. There was nothing special when I took secondary education at the school. I really like swimming. After three years now I’m studying at SMKN 7 Kuningan.

My mother is a teacher at an elementary school, and my father works as an entrepreneur, farming too. I am the first child of 2 siblings, my sister is named Dawn, she is now 13 years old. Oh yes, I forgot, I am 20 years old, I am a student at STKIP Muhammadiyah Kuningan, just in the 3rd semester.

Maybe that's enough introduction from me, warm greetings Toni.
